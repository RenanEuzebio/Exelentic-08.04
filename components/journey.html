Evolution of Digital Automation

  • 1. Business Process Management (BPM)

    Structuring and managing business processes for efficiency.

  • 2. Process Automation (RPA & DPA)

    Automating repetitive tasks through UIs (RPA) and low-code platforms for end-to-end workflows (DPA).

  • 3. Intelligent Automation (IA)

    Enhancing automation with AI to make processes smarter and adaptive.

  • 4. Agentic Automation (APA & AGI)

    Deploying AI agents capable of handling tasks autonomously with minimal or no human input.
